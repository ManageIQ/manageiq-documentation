=== Viewing Reports

Once you have run a report, you can view it at any time after the first scheduled time has occurred.

. Navigate to menu:Overview[Reports].
. Click the *Saved Reports* accordion or the *Reports* accordion.
. Click on the instance of the report you want to view.
